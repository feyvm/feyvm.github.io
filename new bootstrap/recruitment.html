Recruitment

Miami University

Rush a Sorority

The Panhellenic Association at Miami University is comprised of seventeen nationally recognized sororities. Panhellenic strives to bring women together and empower them to be their best selves. Sororities provide individuals with a variety of different programs and competitions. Greek life also participates in various philanthropic events. For more information you can check out the Panhellenic Website.

Participation Eligibility

You must have have earned 12 credit hours at Miami University or another college or university You must have a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5 Eligibility requirements must be met at end of the semester prior to accepting a bid for sorority membership You must be a full time Oxford Campus student during the semester in which you participate in recruitment All rst year women who join a sorority are required to live in the sorority corridor during their second year and must sign an on-campus housing agreement to facilitate that
