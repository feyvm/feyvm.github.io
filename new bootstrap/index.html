Chi Omega

Miami University

Sigma Alpha Chapter of Chi Omega

Miami’s Chapter, Sigma Alpha of Chi Omega, was founded in 1903. Chi Omega is the third oldest sorority on Miami University's campus. With deep roots of history and tradition, our sisterhood grows stronger with time.

History of Chi Omega

Chi Omega was founded in 1985 at the University of Arkansas. It is one of the largest sorority in the world. Chi Omega has many different beliefs and practices. Fun fact the flower of Chi O is the carnation because it is the only flower that grows closer as it dies.

Sisters On Purpose

Our Sisters are committed to Chi Omega’s six purposes. These purposes guide each Chi Omega member and include the following: Friendship, High Standards of Personnel, Sincere Learning and Credible Scholarship, Participation in Campus Activities, Career Development, and Community Service.
